How To Collect Reports In App Store Connect

  1. Sign in to App Store Connect and open Sales and Trends.
  2. Export the Detailed Sales report (filename usually starts with S_D_).
  3. Export the Subscriber report (filename usually starts with Subscriber_).
  4. Save both files as the raw tab-separated .txt format.
  5. Drag both files into the upload area above.
  6. Copy values from the tables into your Finance models if needed.

All processing is client-side only. Files never leave your browser session.
